Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 27, 2025

At AxelBase Offline Barcode Generator, your privacy is not a feature — it’s the entire foundation.

We believe that tools should respect you. That’s why this barcode generator runs 100% in your browser, with zero data collection, no tracking, no analytics, and no servers.

We Collect Nothing. Period.

When you use AxelBase:

  • No IP address is logged
  • No barcode data is transmitted
  • No usage statistics are recorded
  • No cookies are set
  • No localStorage beyond your current session
  • No analytics (not even privacy-friendly ones)

Your product codes, serial numbers, prices, inventory IDs — everything stays on your device.

How It Works

All barcode generation uses bwip-js running locally in your browser. ZIP files are created with JSZip — entirely client-side. When you click “Download”, the file comes directly from your own computer. We never see it.

No Third Parties

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Meta Pixel
  • Cloudflare Insights
  • Any advertising or tracking scripts

The only external resource is the font and CSS from GitHub Pages — nothing that tracks you.

Offline = Ultimate Privacy

After the first visit, AxelBase works completely offline. You can disable the internet, go into airplane mode, or use it in a secure facility — it will still work perfectly.

Self-Hosted Version

If you fork and host AxelBase yourself, you have full control. You can even remove the “AxelBase” name. It becomes your private tool.

Children’s Privacy

This tool is safe for all ages. Since no data is collected, it complies with COPPA, GDPR, CCPA, and every privacy law by default.

Changes to This Policy

We will never weaken this policy. Any update will only strengthen privacy protections.

You don’t need to trust us with your data — because we never ask for it.
This is privacy by design.
