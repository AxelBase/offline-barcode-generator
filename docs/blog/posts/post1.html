Introducing AxelBase

Introducing AxelBase Offline Barcode Generator

Published: November 27, 2025

In an age where every online barcode generator asks for your data, tracks your usage, or requires an account — AxelBase does none of that.

Built from the ground up as a 100% static, client-side web app, AxelBase lets you generate professional-grade barcodes — Code 128, Code 39, EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A, and PDF417 — entirely in your browser. No backend. No database. No telemetry. No data ever leaves your device.

Why We Built This

Most online barcode tools:

  • Log your input
  • Show ads
  • Require sign-up
  • Stop working when the server goes down

AxelBase was created to fix that. Whether you're in a warehouse with no internet, a retail store, or just value your privacy — this tool works anywhere, anytime, forever.

Key Features

  • Generate up to 100 barcodes at once
  • Download all as a clean ZIP with perfect PNGs
  • High-resolution output: 480p up to true 4K
  • White background + padding for perfect scanning
  • Full drag-and-drop reordering and validation
  • Works completely offline after first visit

Perfect For

Warehouse labeling • Retail price tags • Inventory management • Event tickets • Product packaging • Hobbyists • Anyone who values privacy

FAQ

Is AxelBase really free?

Yes — forever. No limits, no sign-up, no ads. Just open and use.

Can I host it myself?

Absolutely. It’s open-source on GitHub and deploys to GitHub Pages in seconds.

Will it work without internet?

Yes — after the first load, it works 100% offline forever.
