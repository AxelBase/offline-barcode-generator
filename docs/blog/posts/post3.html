Bulk Generation Guide

Generate 100 Barcodes in One Click – Here’s How

Published: November 27, 2025

Need 50 price tags? 100 inventory labels? A full product launch batch? AxelBase makes it effortless.

Step-by-Step Bulk Workflow

1. Click “Add Barcode” up to 100 times
2. Enter your data (or paste from Excel)
3. Choose resolution (1080p or 4K for printing)
4. Click “Download All as ZIP”

That’s it. You get a perfectly named ZIP with crisp, print-ready PNGs — all in under 10 seconds.

Pro Tips

  • Use 4K + Custom 6× for thermal printers
  • Drag to reorder before export
  • White background + padding = perfect scanning
  • Name files will be barcode_001.png → barcode_100.png

FAQ

How fast is generation?

100 barcodes @ 1080p takes ~8–12 seconds on average hardware.

Can I automate input?

Yes — paste from spreadsheet or use keyboard shortcuts.
