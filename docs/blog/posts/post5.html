Perfect White Backgrounds and Padding – Pro Tips

Published: November 27, 2025

You’ve generated a beautiful barcode — but it fails to scan. Why? 99% of the time: missing white space.

The “Quiet Zone” Rule

Every barcode needs a blank white border (called a quiet zone) around all sides — minimum 10× the width of the narrowest bar. Without it, scanners get confused.

AxelBase automatically adds:

  • Pure white background
  • 20px padding on all sides
  • Extra padding in high-resolution modes

Why Online Generators Fail

Most web tools output barcodes on transparent or colored backgrounds — fine for screens, terrible for printing. Result: scanning errors at checkout or warehouse.

Best Practices Built In

  • White background (#FFFFFF)
  • Minimum 20px padding (scales with resolution)
  • Sharp 3× scaling for crisp printing
  • PNG format (lossless)

Pro Tip: Resolution Matters

For thermal label printers → use 4K or Custom 6–8×
For laser/inkjet → 1080p is perfect
For mobile display → 720p is sufficient

FAQ

Can I remove the white background?

Not recommended — but you can crop manually after download.

Why PNG and not SVG?

PNG guarantees consistent rendering across all printers and apps.
